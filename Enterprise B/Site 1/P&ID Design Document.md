## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This P&ID Design Document provides a comprehensive engineering specification for Enterprise B - Site 1, a beverage/liquid filling facility. The facility encompasses four primary production areas:

- **Liquid Processing Area** - Batch mixing and intermediate storage
- **Filler Production Area** - Container washing, filling, and capping
- **Packaging Area** - Labeling, case packing, and sealing
- **Palletizing Area** - Automated and manual palletizing with stretch wrapping

The facility operates with:
- 4 mixing vats (VAT-01 through VAT-04)
- 6 storage tanks (TK-01 through TK-06)
- 3 parallel filling lines
- 4 parallel labeler/packaging lines
- 2 automated palletizers + 4 manual palletizing stations

**Design Capacity:** Based on observed filler rates of approximately 300 bottles/min per line, total facility throughput is estimated at 900 bottles/min or 54,000 bottles/hour across all three filling lines operating simultaneously.

## 8. CONTROL LOOP DESCRIPTIONS

### 8.1 Temperature Control Loops

#### TC-101: VAT-01 Temperature Control
- **Controlled Variable:** Product temperature in VAT-01
- **Setpoint:** Recipe dependent (typical 20-25 degC)
- **Manipulated Variable:** Glycol valve position (TV-101)
- **Control Action:** Reverse (increase output = more cooling)
- **Control Algorithm:** PID
- **Tuning Parameters:** P=2.0, I=120s, D=30s

```
                    +--------+
    TT-101 -------->|  TIC   |-------> TV-101
    (4-20 mA)       |  101   |        (Glycol Valve)
                    +--------+
                        ^
                        |
                    Setpoint
                    (Recipe)
```

#### TC-102 through TC-104: Similar configuration for VAT-02 through VAT-04

### 8.2 Level Control Loops

#### LC-201: TK-01 Level Control
- **Controlled Variable:** Tank level
- **Setpoint:** Operator adjustable (typical 60-80%)
- **Manipulated Variable:** Discharge pump speed (P-201) via VFD
- **Control Action:** Direct (increase output = faster discharge)
- **Control Algorithm:** PI
- **Tuning Parameters:** P=1.5, I=60s

### 8.3 Flow Control Loops

#### FC-201: TK-01 to Filler Supply Flow
- **Controlled Variable:** Product flow rate to filling lines
- **Setpoint:** Based on filler demand signal
- **Manipulated Variable:** Flow control valve position (FV-201)
- **Control Action:** Direct
- **Control Algorithm:** PID with feedforward from filler rate

### 8.4 Cascade Control: Tank Level to Flow

```
                           +--------+
    LT-201 --------------->|  LIC   |----+
    (Tank Level)           |  201   |    |
                           +--------+    |
                               ^         | Setpoint
                               |         v
                           Operator   +--------+
                           Setpoint   |  FIC   |-------> FV-201
                                      |  201   |
                           +--------->+--------+
                           |
                        FT-201
                    (Discharge Flow)
```

### 8.5 Filling Line Speed Coordination

The three filling lines operate with coordinated speed control to balance production:

- **Master:** Line 301 (or highest priority active line)
- **Followers:** Lines 302, 303 ratio-controlled to master
- **Ratio adjustment:** Based on downstream packaging capacity
